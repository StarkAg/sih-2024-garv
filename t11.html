Our Solution

GARV is a cutting-edge solution designed to address women's safety concerns by leveraging advanced gesture recognition technology. Our system is capable of detecting potential threats in real-time, providing immediate alerts and ensuring rapid response.

Technology Behind GARV

GARV utilizes state-of-the-art algorithms and camera technology to detect specific gestures that are indicative of potential threats. This innovative approach minimizes false alarms and ensures that only genuine threats are identified.

  • Advanced Gesture Recognition: Our system identifies aggressive gestures, physical altercations, and other signs of danger.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: The system operates continuously, ensuring that no threat goes unnoticed.
  • Instant Alerts: When a threat is detected, GARV sends immediate alerts to predefined contacts and emergency services.

How GARV Works

The GARV system is easy to set up and use:



  • Installation: Place GARV cameras in strategic locations for maximum coverage.
  • Configuration: Set up the system to recognize specific threat-indicating gestures.
  • Continuous Monitoring: GARV monitors the environment in real-time, ready to detect any potential threats.
  • Alert and Response: When a threat is detected, alerts are immediately sent to relevant parties for prompt action.
